About me


I'm Paul Cervantes, a Senior Visual Designer with 10+ years of experience working with a wide range of industries like banking, transportation, law, travel, environmental research, payment services, gaming, sports, education, and tech.

 

I have worked at companies like BBVA, Royal Caribbean, and HSBC. I am a highly skilled User Interface designer with a passion to deliver the best user experiences.

 

I love working on multi-disciplinary teams, when I'm not designing I love traveling with my wife, surfing and skateboarding.
